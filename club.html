Hellenic Wine Club

The Reserve Wines from Our Vineyard

2 times a year, Hellenic Wine Club members receive 2 or 4 bottles of the "Reserve" wines, carefully selected by our Winemaker, Jaques St. Jean.

In addition to receiving our Reserve wines, members are invited to exclusive club events, including harvest parties, Winemaker dinners, and member-only concerts in our Walla Walla vineyard.

  • 25% off all wine purchases.
  • 30% discount on any full case purchases (Yes you can mix and match!)
  • 15% off all events for club members.
  • Complimentary reserve tasting for four annually (by appointment)
  • Latest updates about the newest releases, harvest updates and upcoming events.
